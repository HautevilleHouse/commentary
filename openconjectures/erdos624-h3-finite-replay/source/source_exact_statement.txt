For X={0,1,2}, determine H(3), the least m for which there exists a function
f:P(X)->X such that every Y subseteq X with |Y|>=m satisfies
{f(A): A subseteq Y}=X. Exhaustive enumeration shows H(3)=2. This is only the
finite H(3) slice and does not settle the asymptotic conjecture.
