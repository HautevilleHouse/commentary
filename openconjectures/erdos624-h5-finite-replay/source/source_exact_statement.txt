For X={0,1,2,3,4}, determine H(5), the least m for which there is a function
f:P(X)->X such that every Y subseteq X with |Y|>=m satisfies
{f(A): A subseteq Y}=X. Return an explicit 32-entry truth table for f and a
complete finite lower-bound certificate for m-1. Preserve the exact quantifier
over all Y and the powerset domain; do not substitute a reciprocal-sum or
geometric statement.
