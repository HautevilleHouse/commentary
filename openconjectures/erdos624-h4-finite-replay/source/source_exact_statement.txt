For X={0,1,2,3}, H(4) is the least m for which there exists a function
f:P(X)->X such that for every Y subseteq X with |Y|>=m, the image
{f(A): A subseteq Y} equals X. Determine H(4). Return an explicit truth table
for f attaining the value and a complete finite proof that m-1 is impossible.
Keep the all-subsets quantifier and the exact definition of H unchanged.
