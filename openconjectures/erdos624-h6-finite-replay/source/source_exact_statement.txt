For X={0,1,2,3,4,5}, determine the least m for which there exists a function
f:P(X)->X such that for every Y subseteq X with |Y|>=m, the image
{f(A): A subseteq Y} equals X. Preserve the full powerset domain and all
quantifiers. This is only the finite H(6) slice and does not settle the
asymptotic Erdős-624 conjecture.
